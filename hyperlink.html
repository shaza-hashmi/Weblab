Holi

Holi is an ancient Hindu festival originating in India and Nepal, known as the "Festival of Colours," celebrating the arrival of spring and the triumph of good over evil. The two-day festival involves lighting bonfires on the first evening (Holika Dahan) to symbolize the burning of evil, followed by a day of joyful celebrations where people smear each other with coloured powders and water, dance, sing, and enjoy traditional sweets. The festival also marks a time for repairing relationships and fostering unity and love among people of all ages and backgrounds. .

Onam

Onam is Kerala's biggest annual harvest and home-coming festival, celebrating the mythical return of King Mahabali to his land. The ten-day festival, held in the Malayalam month of Chingam (August-September), involves elaborate decorations, traditional Pookalam (flower carpets), a grand feast called Onasadya, and lively cultural performances like the Vallamkali boat race and tiger dances.
